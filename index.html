Missions

SpaceX designs, manufactures and launches the world's most advanced rockets and spacecraft. SpaceX has gained worldwide attention for a series of historic milestones.

  • Mission 1: First privately funded, liquid-fueled rocket to reach orbit
  • Mission 2: First privately funded company to successfully launch, orbit, and recover a spacecraft
  • Mission 3: First privately funded company to send a spacecraft to the International Space Station

Launches

SpaceX launches spacecraft to deliver cargo to the International Space Station, carry satellites into orbit, and even launch astronauts into space.

  • Falcon 1: First privately developed liquid-fueled rocket to reach Earth orbit
  • Falcon 9: First reusable rocket
  • Falcon Heavy: Most powerful operational rocket in the world

Vehicles

SpaceX is developing a family of launch vehicles and spacecraft that will dramatically reduce the cost of space access.

  • Falcon 9: Reliable and reusable
  • Dragon: Cargo and crew transportation
  • Starship: Future of space travel and colonization

About Us

Founded in 2002 by Elon Musk, SpaceX's mission is to enable humans to become a spacefaring civilization and a multi-planet species by building a self-sustaining city on Mars.

SpaceX is developing the technologies to make this possible, with the ultimate goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars.
